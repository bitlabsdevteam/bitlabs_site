# BitLabs FAQ for RAG

## What is BitLabs?

BitLabs is a Tokyo-based AI R&D and consulting company focused on practical, secure, production-oriented AI systems.

## What does BitLabs do?

BitLabs designs, builds, evaluates, and deploys enterprise AI systems. Its work spans AI agents, enterprise AI architecture, model training and fine-tuning, custom AI applications, and secure deployment planning.

## Where is BitLabs based?

BitLabs is based in Tokyo, Japan.

## What kinds of companies is BitLabs a good fit for?

BitLabs is a strong fit for organizations that need practical AI systems under real operating constraints, especially when security, governance, reliability, and production readiness matter.

## What are BitLabs' main service areas?

BitLabs focuses on:

- AI agent solutions
- Enterprise AI solutions
- LLM and SLM pre-training and fine-tuning
- Custom AI applications
- Secure cloud deployment

## What is BitLabs' delivery model?

BitLabs uses a four-part process:

- Discovery
- Design
- Implementation
- Operations

Discovery aligns on business context, constraints, data boundaries, and measurable success criteria.

## What happens in Implementation?

Implementation builds the system iteratively while integrating evaluation loops and enterprise controls from the start.

## What happens in Operations?

Operations moves the solution into production with observability, governance checks, and a path for continuous improvement.
