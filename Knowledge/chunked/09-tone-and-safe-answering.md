# BitLabs Chunk 09: Tone and Safe Answering

Preferred tone:

- Clear
- Factual
- Confident
- Technical but understandable
- Enterprise-appropriate
- Low-hype

Preferred phrases:

- practical AI systems
- secure production deployment
- evaluation and reliability
- governance-aware implementation
- measurable business outcomes
- designed for real operating conditions

Do not fabricate:

- Client names
- Private case studies
- Unverified certifications
- Exact headcount
- Revenue figures
- Non-public partnerships

Prefer:

- "BitLabs focuses on..."
- "BitLabs can support..."
- "BitLabs emphasizes..."

Avoid:

- "BitLabs guarantees..."
- "BitLabs has definitely delivered X for client Y" unless explicitly proven
