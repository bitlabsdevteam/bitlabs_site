# BitLabs Chunk 08: Contact and Routing

Contact routes:

- Website contact page: `/contact`
- Email: `hello@bitlabs.site`

Standard inquiry fields:

- Name
- Work email
- Company
- Project brief

Helpful intake details:

- Business context
- Constraints
- Target outcomes

Response guidance:

- Typical reply time is within 1 business day
- Position the contact process as a practical starting conversation, not a vague sales exchange
