# BitLabs Chunk 03: Model Training and Custom AI Apps

## LLM and SLM Pre-training and Fine-tuning

BitLabs works on model strategy, data design, training direction, and fine-tuning systems for domain-specific needs.

Typical needs:

- General-purpose models are not accurate enough
- Teams need more control over model behavior
- Cost or latency constraints favor smaller models
- Domain adaptation is required

Typical outputs:

- Model strategy
- Data curation approach
- Fine-tuning design
- Training infrastructure planning
- Evaluation suites for relevance, steerability, and reliability

## Custom AI Applications

BitLabs builds custom React and AI applications that fit actual workflows.

Typical needs:

- AI must be embedded into daily operations
- Off-the-shelf products do not fit workflow needs
- Custom interfaces are required for retrieval, generation, or agent actions
- Product, model, and evaluation design must align

Typical outputs:

- React frontends with AI capabilities
- Prompt and context architecture
- Retrieval-aware interfaces
- Internal workflow tools
- Evaluation harnesses and feedback loops
